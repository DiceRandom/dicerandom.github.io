  • Pretty Women Kaitlyn B

    This movie portrays a fairytale with a twist. Vivian, as a prostitute, never believed in love until she met billionaire Edward. Vivian was requested by Edward to stay for a week. All that had been in Vivians mind was that she was going to have sex with Edward with no connection, as she usually does with other clients. Pretty Woman is sure to keep your emotions running high, whether it’s anger, sadness, happiness, or pure bliss. This movie will touch every viewer's heart. The climax will keep you at the edge of your seat, but it’s worth it at the end. From start to finish this is a 10/10 movie and I strongly recommend it for anyone (especially hopeless romantics like myself).
